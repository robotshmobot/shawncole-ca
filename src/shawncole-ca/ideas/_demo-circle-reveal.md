Teal and gold is an old combination. It shows up in Art Deco, in mosque tilework, in vintage book covers. The pairing feels both ancient and contemporary. Source Serif 4 titles carry the gold color with weight. Geist body text keeps the prose grounded.

## How it feels

Elegant. Assured. The teal-gold palette has enough contrast to read well while feeling intentionally restrained. Neither color shouts. They complement each other the way a frame complements a painting.

> "Perfection is achieved when there is nothing left to take away."

Good for pieces that need to feel polished without feeling corporate. The palette signals taste and restraint. Pair it with strong imagery or let the typography and color do everything.
