The entire viewport drowns in a single color. Content floats in it. There are no containers, no cards, no borders. The field is the design.

This vibe works best when the color does the talking. Pick something bold. Let the text sit quietly inside it.

## How it feels

Immersive. Like stepping into a room painted floor to ceiling in one color. The reader's eye has nowhere to go but the words.

> "Color is a power which directly influences the soul."

Good for manifestos, short fiction, opinion pieces, anything with conviction. Bad for anything that needs visual complexity or multiple sections competing for attention.
