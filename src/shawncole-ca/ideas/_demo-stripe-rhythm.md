Purple sits between warm and cool. The deep violet background creates a contemplative atmosphere. Warm off-white text softens the contrast. Source Serif 4 titles bring gravity. Geist body text keeps it readable.

## How it feels

Regal. Meditative. Purple historically signals luxury and introspection. The serif-sans pairing at its most traditional grounds the color choice in something familiar.

> "Rhythm is the basis of life, not steady forward progress."

Good for pieces that need to feel considered and deliberate. The purple won't fight for attention the way red or orange would. It just sits there, quietly commanding the room.
