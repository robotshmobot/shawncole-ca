Orange radiates heat. The burnt orange field feels like late afternoon sun. White text cuts through cleanly. SRC Marker titles in uppercase bring a handmade quality. Source Serif 4 body text provides the reading backbone.

## How it feels

Warm. Approachable. Handwritten. The hand-serif pairing combines the looseness of SRC Marker with the formality of Source Serif 4. The orange amplifies the warmth already present in the hand font.

> "The medium is the message."

Hand-serif works for personal essays, reflections, and pieces where the author's voice should feel present in the typography itself. The irregular letterforms signal a human behind the words.
