Black is the absence of decision. The darkest field lets everything else come forward. Mono-sans titles in uppercase feel architectural. Geist body text is clean and neutral.

## How it feels

Stark. Confident. The black background says there's nothing to hide behind. No color choice to distract from the content. The writing stands or falls on its own.

> "Architecture is frozen music."

Use black when the content is strong enough to carry the page alone. Weak writing on a black background feels like a missing poster. Strong writing on black feels like a gallery wall.
