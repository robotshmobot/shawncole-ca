Almost white but not quite. The fafafa background has just enough warmth to distinguish it from a blank page. Geist Mono titles and Geist body text. Everything uppercase in the headers, clean and systematic everywhere else.

## How it feels

Clinical. Stripped back. The near-white field disappears, putting all attention on the typography and content structure. The mono-sans pairing reinforces the systems aesthetic.

> "Information is cheap. Meaning is expensive."

This combination works for content where the writing should feel like documentation with personality. Technical enough to be credible, designed enough to be interesting.
