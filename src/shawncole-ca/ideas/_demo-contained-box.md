Warm and quiet. The background recedes, letting the typography do the work. Source Serif 4 in the titles, Geist in the body. The combination reads like a well-set book page.

## How it feels

Considered. Literate. The parchment tone adds warmth without demanding attention. Good for long-form writing where comfort matters more than impact.

> "Every frame is a decision about what matters."

The serif-sans pairing is the most traditional option in the system. Use it when the content should feel authoritative rather than experimental.
