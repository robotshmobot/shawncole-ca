Red hits different. The SRC Marker font in the titles makes the whole thing feel hand-lettered, urgent, like someone grabbed a marker and started writing on a painted wall.

## How it feels

Energetic. Impulsive. The hand font breaks any sense of corporate polish. Combined with the red, it reads as protest art or a zine cover.

> "Design is the conscious effort to impose meaningful order."

The hand-sans pairing works best when the content has personality. Formal analysis or dry technical writing will fight against the looseness of the letterforms.
