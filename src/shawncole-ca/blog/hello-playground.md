Welcome to the playground.

This site isn't trying to be anything in particular yet. It's a space for ideas to stretch their legs, bump into each other, and see what happens.

## Why a playground?

Because portfolios are boring. Because blogs feel like obligations. Because the best work happens when you stop trying to make it look like work.

> "The creation of something new is not accomplished by the intellect but by the play instinct."

So here we are. A weird little corner of the internet that doesn't take itself too seriously.

## What to expect

- Writing that wanders
- Projects that might not ship
- Experiments in form and content
- The occasional thing that actually works

Stay curious.
